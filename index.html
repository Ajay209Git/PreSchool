About Us

Bright Start Preschool provides a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for children aged 2-5 years. Our mission is to foster curiosity, creativity, and a love of learning in every child.

  • Qualified, caring teachers
  • Play-based curriculum
  • Healthy snacks and meals
  • Safe indoor and outdoor play areas

Our Programs

Playgroup (2-3 yrs)

Introduction to social, emotional, and motor skills through fun activities and play.

Nursery (3-4 yrs)

Focus on language development, basic math concepts, and creativity through art and music.

Kindergarten (4-5 yrs)

Preparation for school with emphasis on reading, writing, math, and problem-solving skills.
